My Journey

I am a student who began my web development journey with absolutely no prior knowledge of programming. In the beginning, even basic coding concepts felt unfamiliar and challenging. My direction became clear when I started learning under the mentorship of Nazar Muhammad , a Software Engineer professionally based in Karachi, Pakistan.

Under his step-by-step guidance, I was introduced to W3Schools to build a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. To deepen my understanding, I was encouraged to learn from Code With Harry and explore selected tutorials from Apna College. These resources helped me strengthen my logical thinking, understand development structure, and apply concepts in practical projects.

Through continuous mentorship, structured learning, and consistent practice, I gradually transformed from a complete beginner into a developer capable of building real-world and community-based projects. More importantly, I learned how to think like a developer — focusing on clean code, problem-solving, and professional execution rather than just writing syntax.

Today, as a student and growing web developer, I remain committed to continuous improvement, skill development, and building meaningful digital solutions that create real impact.
